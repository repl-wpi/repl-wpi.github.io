We create languages, libraries, and environments for creative audiovisual coding, and publish our work as open-source software. We research how to make these tools user-friendly, efficient, and artistically interesting along the way. Lab members are faculty, students, and alumni from Worcester Polytechnic Institute’s Interactive Media & Game Development Program and/or Computer Science Department.
